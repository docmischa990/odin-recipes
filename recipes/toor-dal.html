Toor Dal

Description

Toor Dal is a staple dish in many Indian households, loved for its hearty flavor and nutritious properties. This comforting dish pairs wonderfully with rice or flatbreads and is packed with spices that create a delicious balance of flavors.

Ingredients

  • 150-200g Toor Dal (washed and soaked overnight)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp haldi (turmeric powder)
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1/2 tsp cumin seeds (jeera)
  • 1/2 tsp minced garlic
  • 1/2 tsp chopped green or red chili
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp sugar

Steps

  1. Wash and soak 150-200g of Toor Dal overnight.
  2. Boil the soaked dal with 1 tsp salt and 1 tsp haldi until soft and fully cooked.
  3. In a frying pan, heat 1 tbsp oil and 1 tbsp butter over medium heat.
  4. Add 1 tsp mustard seeds and wait for them to crackle.
  5. Add 1/2 tsp cumin seeds, 1/2 tsp minced garlic, and 1/2 tsp chopped green or red chili. Saut√© briefly.
  6. Stir in 1/2 tsp haldi, 1 tsp coriander powder, and 1/2 tsp red chili powder. Mix well.
  7. Pour the tempered mixture into the boiling dal and stir to combine.
  8. Add 1/2 tsp sugar to the dal, then boil and mix well to incorporate all the flavors.
  9. Serve hot with rice or flatbreads for a hearty and flavorful meal.
