Ragi Barley Soup

Description

Ragi barley soup is a wholesome and nutritious dish made from ragi flour, which is derived from crushed mixed grains. This hearty soup is mildly sweet, rich in flavor, and perfect for a nourishing breakfast or evening snack.

Traditionally, jaggery and coconut milk are used to enhance the taste, making it a delightful and energy-packed meal.

Ingredients

  • 500 ml water
  • 8 tbsp ragi flour
  • Room temperature water (for mixing the flour)
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 small pyramid of jaggery
  • 1 cup coconut milk (or any preferred milk)

Steps

  1. Bring 500 ml of water to a boil in a large pan.
  2. In a separate bowl, mix 8 tablespoons of ragi flour with room temperature water to form a smooth paste without lumps.
  3. Gradually add the ragi paste to the boiling water, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  4. Cook the mixture on low heat for 10-15 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. Add a pinch of salt and one pyramid-shaped jaggery piece. Stir until the jaggery dissolves completely.
  6. Pour in the coconut milk (or your preferred milk) and let the soup come to a gentle boil.
  7. Serve warm and enjoy!
