Semolina Porridge

Description

Semolina porridge is a comforting and delicious breakfast dish that is quick and easy to prepare. It's creamy, slightly sweet, and perfect for starting the day on a warm note.

This traditional dish is popular in many cultures and can be customized with various toppings like nuts, fruits, or honey to suit your taste.

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 5 heaped tbsp semolina (ravo)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 cup milk
  • 3 tbsp sugar
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

  1. Heat the butter in a pan and lightly fry the semolina (ravo) until golden and aromatic.
  2. Gradually add water and milk, stirring continuously to avoid lumps.
  3. Reduce the heat to low and add the sugar. Keep stirring until the mixture thickens to your desired consistency.
  4. Add a pinch of salt to balance the sweetness.
  5. Optional: Top with nuts, dried fruits, or a drizzle of honey before serving.
