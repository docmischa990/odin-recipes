Crepes

Description

Crepes are thin and delicate pancakes that can be served sweet or savory. They are versatile, easy to make, and perfect for breakfast, brunch, or dessert.

This recipe creates light and fluffy crepes that can be paired with your favorite toppings like fruits, chocolate, or cheese.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 1/4 cup (310 ml) milk
  • 2 tbsp melted butter

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, sift together the flour, baking powder, and salt.
  2. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs and sugar until well combined.
  3. Gradually add the milk to the egg mixture, whisking continuously to incorporate.
  4. Slowly pour the wet mixture into the dry ingredients, whisking until a smooth batter forms.
  5. Stir in the melted butter to complete the batter.
  6. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium heat and lightly grease it with butter or oil.
  7. Pour a small amount of batter into the skillet, tilting it to spread the batter evenly into a thin layer.
  8. Cook until the edges begin to lift and the bottom is lightly golden, about 1-2 minutes.
  9. Flip the crepe and cook for an additional 1 minute until fully set.
  10. Repeat with the remaining batter, stacking the cooked crepes on a plate.
  11. Serve warm with your favorite toppings.
