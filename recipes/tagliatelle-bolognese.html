Tagliatelle Bolognese

Description

Tagliatelle Bolognese is a rich and hearty Italian classic. This dish combines tender tagliatelle pasta with a savory meat sauce made from minced mutton, aromatic vegetables, and a touch of wine and herbs. Perfect for a comforting meal any day of the week!

Ingredients

Serves 3

  • 1 packet tagliatelle
  • 40 g butter
  • 70 ml olive oil
  • 25–30 g bacon, finely chopped (optional)
  • 2 medium onions, finely chopped
  • 7–8 cloves garlic, minced
  • 100 g carrot, peeled and finely chopped/grated
  • 1 stick celery, finely chopped
  • 500 g minced mutton (lean meat preferred)
  • 150 ml good red wine
  • 100 ml milk
  • 400 g canned tomatoes, chopped (with juices)
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 1 handful basil (torn) or thyme
  • Salt & pepper to taste
  • 1 red bell pepper (roasted, peeled, and finely chopped; optional)

Steps

  1. In a heavy saucepan or non-stick pan, melt the butter and heat the olive oil over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onions and cook for 3-4 minutes until softened.
  3. Stir in the celery, garlic, and carrot. Cook for another 2 minutes.
  4. Add the minced mutton and mix well with the vegetables. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. Pour in the red wine and increase the heat to high. Cook until the liquid evaporates.
  6. Add the milk and cook again until the liquid evaporates.
  7. Stir in the canned tomatoes with their juice, basil or thyme, and bay leaf. Reduce the heat to low and let the sauce simmer for about an hour, stirring occasionally.
  8. If using, add the finely chopped roasted red bell pepper to the sauce for extra flavor.
  9. Meanwhile, cook the tagliatelle according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  10. Once the sauce is ready, toss it with the cooked tagliatelle. Serve warm with grated Parmesan cheese, if desired.
