Chicken Korma with Quinoa

Description

Chicken Korma is a rich and creamy dish that’s both flavorful and satisfying. This version uses a preset marinade for convenience and pairs the chicken with cooked quinoa for a nutritious and hearty meal.

Ingredients

For the Chicken Marinade (Serves 2)

  • 600 g chicken breast (boneless), diced
  • 1/2 cup korma marinade (store-bought)

For the Stir-Fry

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • The marinated chicken
  • 1 medium onion, chopped
  • 1 red bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1-2 green chilies, sliced
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp red chili powder (adjust to taste)
  • Optional: 2 tbsp yogurt or cream for extra creaminess
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

For the Quinoa

  • 100 g quinoa
  • 200 ml boiling water
  • 1 tbsp butter or olive oil
  • Pinch of salt

Steps

    Marinating the Chicken

  1. In a bowl, combine the diced chicken breast with the korma marinade. Mix well and let it sit for at least 30 minutes.

    2. Preparing the Stir-Fry

  2. Heat 2 tbsp of oil in a large frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Add the marinated chicken and cook until browned and nearly done. Remove from the pan and set aside.
  4. In the same pan, sauté the chopped onion until soft and translucent.
  5. Add the red and green bell peppers, green chilies, turmeric powder, cumin powder, coriander powder, and red chili powder. Stir-fry for 3-4 minutes.
  6. Return the chicken to the pan and mix well with the vegetables and spices.
  7. If desired, add 2 tbsp of yogurt or cream to make the dish creamier. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  8. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves before serving.

    9. Preparing the Quinoa

  9. Rinse the quinoa under cold water to remove bitterness.
  10. In a small saucepan, bring 200 ml of water to a boil.
  11. Add the quinoa and a pinch of salt. Cover and reduce the heat to low.
  12. Simmer for 12-15 minutes or until the water is fully absorbed and the quinoa is fluffy.
  13. Stir in 1 tbsp of butter or olive oil before serving.
